### **Must-Know Tools in Blender:**
**Essential 20% (80% Results):**
- **Modeling:** Loop cuts, extrusion, bevel
- **Modifiers:** Array, mirror, subdivision surface
- **Materials:** Principled BSDF shader
- **Lighting:** Sun lamp, area lights, HDRI
- **Animation:** Camera animation, graph editor basics
- **Compositing:** Denoising, glare, color correction
